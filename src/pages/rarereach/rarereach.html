Rare Reach Step 1: Connect with RGI

After contacting Rare Genomics Institute, you will receive a link to an application form to provide relevant medical information and consent for RG's network.

 Step 2: Talk to a Patient Advocate

Your Patient Advocate will set-up an informational phone call to educate you about the process.
After the call, you will be asked to fill out a follow-up form to familiarize you with the process and find you the most fitting RG partner.

 Step 3: Physician Referral

A physician must request exome or whole genome sequencing.

A clinical geneticist must determine your eligibility for sequencing, and the physician will interpret sequencing results.

 Step 4: Connect to a RG Partner

Once your Patient Advocate(PA) receives sequencing confirmation from your physician, PA will give you requisition forms and shipping information to request sequencing with the RG partner laboratory.

 Step 5: Funding

RG offers a few options for genome sequencing:

  1. Self-Pay

  2. Insurance

  3. Crowdfunding
